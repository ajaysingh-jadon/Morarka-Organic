FoodSpert – Knowledge Share

Sharing food wisdom, science and tradition to build a healthier future.

Food is knowledge, joy and a way of life.

Having defined a Foodspert as someone who knows about food in its entirety and for whom food is also one form of ultimate joy & happiness in life, we are now looking to make this a global movement.

We are looking for those who are Foodsperts themselves and are also willing to inspire more and more people to become Foodsperts.

Today almost every one eats food with a sense of guilt; be it due to health problems already suffered by a person or likely to afflict a person in near future. A large percentage of our society is now looking for solutions to this problem. Alternatives in the form of healthy salads & soups are not the perfectly acceptable solutions. Many of us have unanswered questions like:

Why Do We Feel Guilty About Food?

  • Why is my regular food not healthy?
  • How did our ancestors eat the same food and remain healthy?
  • Why do healthy choices always seem to compromise on taste?

We Have the Answers

We want to share the wealth of information and experiences we have gathered over the years. Today, accessing correct and meaningful information about food has become increasingly difficult.

In earlier days, we acquired knowledge inherited through generations and through our own experiences. Today it is becoming increasingly difficult to access correct information and true facts about food. Traditional media has limited content, mostly recipes and horror stories about contamination and adulteration in foods.
