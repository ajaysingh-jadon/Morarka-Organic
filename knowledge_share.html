Why Do We Feel Guilty About Food?

Modern food is often produced using excessive chemical inputs, artificial fertilizers, pesticides and preservatives, which reduce its nutritional quality and affect long-term health.

Earlier generations consumed food grown with traditional practices, native seeds, natural soil fertility and minimal processing, which preserved both nutrition and taste.

Highly processed foods are engineered for instant flavour, while real, natural food develops taste through soil quality, traditional varieties and slow, mindful cultivation.
