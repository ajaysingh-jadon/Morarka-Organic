Strategic Evolution

Activities & Operations

From Direct Procurement to National Network

Morarka Organic Food Limited began with direct procurement of organic produce from farms and its supply to organic trade partners. Over time, operations expanded to include year-round storage, enabling greater aggregation of farm-level produce to meet market demand. Responding to the growing need for processed organic foods, MOFL established in-house and partner processing facilities and subsequently developed its own distribution and retail network across India.

From an initial range of select cereals, pulses, oilseeds, and spices, MOFL today offers a diversified portfolio of over 170 certified organic products.

Integrated Approach

Cultivation to Value Chain Management

Complete Value Chain Integration

With strong technical expertise in organic cultivation, MOFL has successfully introduced organic farming across multiple agro-climatic zones of India. To meet rising demand for value-added products, the company strengthened its value chain management capabilities by developing farmer-friendly tools, small-scale processing solutions, and in-house technologies such as CO₂ fumigation for extended organic storage.

Focused on delivering certified organic produce at affordable prices, MOFL continues to enhance its packaging and logistics solutions to ensure quality and shelf stability.

Strategic Alliances

Collaborations & Entrepreneurial Partnerships

Building a Collaborative Ecosystem

Anchored in a public-private partnership approach, MOFL has built strategic collaborations with individuals and organizations at both national and international levels. A unique model was developed to engage educated, unemployed youth from producer communities to establish and manage key value-chain functions including cultivation, certification, procurement, processing, storage, distribution, and retail.

Additionally, partnerships with high-capacity and high-quality processing units have strengthened operational efficiency. Within the first five years, MOFL successfully collaborated with over 300 entrepreneurs across India, significantly expanding its organic footprint.

Foundation Strength

Infrastructure Backbone

Robust Infrastructure for Sustainable Growth

Backed by the Morarka Foundation—an outcome of corporate philanthropy by Gannon Dunkerley & Company Ltd. and the philanthropic vision of Mr. Kamal Morarka—Morarka Organic Food Limited benefits from a strong, self-sustained infrastructure. From its inception, the organization invested in skilled human resources, robust facilities, and appropriate technologies to ensure efficient and scalable operations.

As the front-end business arm of the Foundation, Morarka Organic leverages this shared infrastructure to provide effective market linkages to organic farmers across India.

Offices & Field Presence

Morarka Organic's headquarters is located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, offering a blend of modern infrastructure and a culturally rooted work environment. The organization operates from a 20,000 sq. ft. office campus spread across approximately 2.5 acres on the outskirts of Jaipur.

Innovation Hub

Research & Development Excellence

Field-Based R&D Philosophy

Research and Development is a cornerstone of Morarka Organic's success. Over 70% of internal resources have been invested in developing innovative, field-tested solutions—many of which are first-of-their-kind in organic agriculture. Rather than laboratory-bound trials, all R&D is conducted directly on farmers' fields, enabling rapid validation, scalability, and adoption.

Over the last decade, Morarka Organic has developed organic cultivation practices for 180+ crops across nearly 10 agro-climatic zones of India, establishing itself as a leading resource organization in the sector.

Biotechnology for Agriculture

To address challenges related to soil fertility, pest management, and sustainable inputs, Morarka Organic established an in-house biotechnology laboratory. Research focuses on understanding natural processes and applying biotechnology-based solutions in real farming conditions:

  • Advanced vermiculture and enrichment processes
  • Plant-extract–based probiotic pest management
  • Scientific waste management solutions
  • Development of CO₂ fumigation technology for large-scale organic storage
  • Chemical-free farm-level storage using airtight metal bins and masonry units
  • Innovation in solar-based dehydration and crop-specific processing technologies
  • 30+ processing technologies developed in last three years

The laboratory was later expanded into two divisions—one dedicated to agricultural research and the other to quality management of organic food products. Every product marketed by Morarka Organic undergoes rigorous quality checks. The labs are supported by qualified professionals and biotechnology trainees from leading institutions.

Digital Transformation

Technology & Systems

Information Technology Systems

Managing large-scale organic farming operations required advanced digital solutions. Morarka Organic developed a custom IT platform to manage farm records, certification compliance, procurement, quality control, and traceability—meeting international organic certification standards.

This system enabled the introduction of group certification models and end-to-end product traceability. Over time, the platform has been enhanced and is now also used by other organizations. A unique initiative trains rural women to digitize farm records, creating local employment opportunities.

Entrepreneurial Infrastructure

Morarka Organic follows an entrepreneur-led model, with nearly 90% of procurement, storage, and processing infrastructure owned and managed by local entrepreneurs. Field teams provide training and technical support for aggregation centres, weighing, packaging, labelling, and logistics.

Centralized storage facilities in Jaipur currently handle approximately 2,000 metric tons, supported by both cold storage and conventional godowns. Processing operations are carried out through a mix of in-house facilities and specialized partner units.

Market Leadership

Marketing, Distribution & Retail

Dual Market Strategy

Morarka Organic serves both bulk and retail markets. Bulk supplies are dispatched directly from Jaipur, while retail operations are currently managed through Jaipur, Mumbai, and Delhi. These hubs supply Morarka Organic's own stores, modern retail chains, dealers, and home-delivery networks.

With expanding demand in retail and e-commerce segments, additional depot locations are being planned to strengthen national distribution.
