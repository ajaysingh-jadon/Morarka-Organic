Careers at Morarka Organic

Initially being slow paced, now the rapid expansion of organic program and its business expansion have created the need for very large number of HR. At Morarka Organic it is now being run like a socially responsible business unit with very high level of professionalization. It has put in place an organizational hierarchy of professionals at various levels.

Beginning with just a few cereals, pulses, oil seeds and seed spices, today Morarka Organic has a portfolio of over 170 different products.

Organizational Hierarchy

Morarka Foundation has implemented a robust professional structure with five entry levels, ensuring clarity, growth, and career advancement.

Relationships

Realizing the need to establish mutually beneficial relationships with interested individuals, organizations and agencies, Morarka Organic finds it has many options.

Any organization and/or individual already engaged in any area related to agriculture, food, health, nutrition, information technology, food processing, lifestyle, marketing, etc. whether already in the organic or not are invited to join hands with us.

Morarka Organic offers many different types of associations such as sharing of information on voluntary basis being at a very primary level, to revenues, investments and profit sharing arrangements.

Knowledge Share

We are especially looking for knowledge partners in the areas of Technology Development & Value Chain Management in Food Value Chain.
