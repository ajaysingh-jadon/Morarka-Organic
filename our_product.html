Our Organic Portfolio

Product Basket

The 100 plus project locations covering over 19 States and almost all agro climatic zones in India, around 1,00,000 farmers and over 10 years in this sector and is still growing every day has enabled Morarka Organic to gain experience in handling over 300 different agriculture products at the farm level. Due to value chain management solutions all developed through in house research, over 100 crops have been understood fairly well by us.

About 130-150 crops-products currently qualify to be part of our core portfolio and are offered both as farm grade as well as processed and in retail packs. Being new, in the trade we are still not handling all the quantities that get produced as organic at the farm level. As we are progressing in our efforts to establish marketing linkages, we are not only expanding the portfolio on offer, we are also increasing the quantities by every crop season.
